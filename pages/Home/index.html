How COBI works

Utilizing Digital Financial Tools, You Can Achieve Better Financial Management And Decision-Making

Users establish a specific savings goal. The savings goal is set within a defined timeframe. Users specify the frequency or intervals at which they want to save. The software calculates the amount that needs to be saved during each interval. Users connect their bank accounts to the software. The software automatically deducts the calculated savings amount at each specified interval.
